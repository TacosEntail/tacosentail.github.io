True Strike

Divination Cantrip

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Self
Duration: Instantaneous
Components: S, M (a weapon with which you have proficiency and that is worth 1+ CP)

guided by a flash of magical insight, you make on attack with the weapon used in the spell's casting. The attack uses your spellcasting ability for the attack and damage rolls instead of using STR or DEX. If the attack deals damage, it can be Radiant damage or the weapon's normal damage type (your choice).

At Higher Levels. Regardless of damage type chosen, this attack deals extra Radiant when you reach 5th level (1d6), 11th level (2d6), and 17th level (4d6).

Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Source: Player's Handbook 2024
