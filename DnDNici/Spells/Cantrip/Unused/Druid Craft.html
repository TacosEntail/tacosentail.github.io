Druidcraft

Transmutation Cantrip

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: 30 Feet
Duration: Instantaneous
Components: V, S

Whispering to the spirits of nature, you create one of the following effects within range.

Weather Sensor: You create a tiny, harmless sensory effect that predicts what the weather will be at your location for the next 24 hours. The effect might manifest as a golden orb for clear skies, a cloud for rain, failing snowflakes for snow, and so on. This effect presists for 1 round.

Bloom: You instantly make a flower blossom, a seed pod open, or a leaf bud bloom.

Sensory Effect: You create an Instantaneous, harmless sensory effect, such as falling leaves, a puff of wind, the sound of a small animal, or the faint odor of skunk. The effect must fit in a 5 foot cube.

Fire Play: You instantly light or snuff out a candle, a torch, or a small campfire.

Classes: Druid
Source: Player's Handbook
