Chill Touch 2024

Necromancy Cantrip

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Touch
Duration: Instantaneous
Components: V, S

Channeling the chill of the grave, make a melee spell attack against a target within reach. On a hit, the target takes 1d10 Necrotic damage, and it can't regain HP until the end of your next turn.

At Higher Levels. This spell's damage increases by 1d10 when you reach 5th level (2d10), 11th level (3d10), and 17th level (4d10).

Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Source: Player's Handbook 2024
