Virtue (UA)

Abjuration Cantrip

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Touch
Duration: 1 Round
Components: V, S

You touch one creature, imbuing it with vitality. If the target has at least 1 HP, it gains a number of temporary HP equal to 1d4 + your spellcasting ability modifier. The temporary HP are lost when the spell ends.

Classes: Cleric
Source: Unearthed Arcana 36 - Starter Spells
