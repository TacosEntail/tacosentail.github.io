Decompose (HB)

Necromancy Cantrip

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Touch
Duration: 1 Minute
Components: V, S

You reach out and touch the corpse of a creature. Over the next minute, the corpse begins to rapidly decompose, sprouting fungus and moss as it begins to degrade into compost and mulch. An odd-colored flower or two may also spring from the corpse in this time. Applicable requirements for resurrection or speak with dead are unaffected by this decomposition.

Classes: Cleric
