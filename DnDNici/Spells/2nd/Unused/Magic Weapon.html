Magic Weapon

2nd level Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 Bonus Action
Range: Touch
Duration: 1 Hour
Components: V, S

You touch a nonmagical weapon. Until the spell ends, that weapon becomes a magic weapon with a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls. The spell ends early if you cast it again.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd - 5th level the bonus increases to +2. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th+ level the bonus increases to +3.

Classes: Artificer, Paladin, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard
Source: Player's Handbook 2024
