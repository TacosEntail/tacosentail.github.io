Continual Flame

2nd level Evocation

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Touch
Duration: Until Dispelled
Components: V, S, M (ruby dust work 50 gp, which the spell consumes)

A flame, casts bright light in a 20 foot radius and dim light for an additional 20 feet, springs forth from an object that you touch. The effect looks like a regular flame, but it creates no heat and doesn't use oxygen. A continual flame can be coverd or hidden but not smothered or quenched.

Classes: Artificer, Cleric, Druid, Wizard
Source: Xanathar's Guide to Everything
