Arcane Vigor

2nd level Abjuration

Casting Time: 1 Bonus Action
Range: Self
Duration: Instantaneous
Components: V, S

You tap into your life force to heal yourself. Roll up to 2 of your unexpended Hit Dice, and regain a number of HP equal to the roll's total plus your spellcasting ability modifier. Those dice are then expended.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the number of unexpended Hit Dice you can roll increases by one for each slot level above 2nd.

Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Source: Player's Handbook 2024
