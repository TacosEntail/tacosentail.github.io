Branding Smite

2nd level Evocation

Casting Time: 1 Bonus Action, which you take immediately after hitting a target with a melee attack
Range: Self
Duration: 1 Minute
Components: V

As you hit the target, your weapon gleams with astral radiance as you strike. The attack deals an extra 3d6 Radiant damage to the target, which becomes visible if it is invisible, and the target sheds dim light in a 5 foot radius and can't become invisible until the spell ends.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 2nd.

Classes: Paladin
Source: Player's Handbook
