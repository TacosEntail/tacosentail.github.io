Digital Phantom (UA)

2nd level Abjuration (Technomagic)

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Self
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 Hour
Components: V, S, M (a small piece of copper wire)

This spell works to actively hide your presense within a computer system. For the spell's duration, you and any other users you choose on your local network gain a +10 bonus to INT checks to avoid detection by administrators, knowbots, tracking software, and the like. Whenever you and your chosen users leave any computer system you are working in while this spell is in effect, all trace of your previous presence in that system is erased.

Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Source: Unearthed Arcana 7 - Modern Magic
