Gift of Gab

2nd level Enchantment

Casting Time: 1 Reaction, which you take when you speak to another creature
Range: Self
Duration: Instantaneous
Components: V, S, M (2gp, which is consumed as tax for using the spell)

When you cast this spell, you skillfully reshape the memories of listeners in your immediate area, so that each creature of your choice within 5 feet of you forgets everything you said within the last 6 seconds. Those creautres then remember that you actually said the words you speak as the verbal component of the spell.

Classes: Bard, Wizard
Source: Acquisitions Inc.
