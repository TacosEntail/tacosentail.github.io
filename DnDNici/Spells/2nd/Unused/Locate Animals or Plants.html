Locate Animals or Plants

2nd level Divination (Ritual)

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Self
Duration: Instantaneous
Components: V, S, M (a bit of fur from a bloodhound)

Describe or name a specific kind of Beast, Plant creature, or nonmagical plant. Concentrating on the voice of nature in your surroundings, you learn the direction and distance to the closest creature or plant of that kind within 5 miles, if any present.

Classes: Bard, Druid, Ranger
Source: Player's Handbook
