Beast Sense

2nd level Divination (Ritual)

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Touch
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 Hour
Components: S

You touch a willing beast. For the duration of the spell, you can perceive through the Beast's senses as well as your own. When perceiving through the Beast's senses, you benefit from any special senses it has.

Classes: Druid, Ranger
Source: Player's Handbook 2024
