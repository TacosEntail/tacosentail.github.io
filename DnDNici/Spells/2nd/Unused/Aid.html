Aid

2nd level Abjuration

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: 30 Feet
Duration: 8 Hours
Components: V, S, M (a tiny strip of cloth)

Your spell bolsters your allies with toughness and resolve. Choose up to three creatures within range. Each target's HP maximum and current HP increase by 5 for the duration.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, a target's HP increase by an additional 5 for each slot level above 2nd.

Classes: Artificer, Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Ranger
Source: Player's Handbook
