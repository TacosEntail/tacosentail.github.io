Invisibility

2nd level Illusion

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Touch
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 Hour
Components: V, S, M (an eyelash encased in gum arabic)

A creature you touch becomes invisible until the spell ends. Anything the target is wearing or carrying is invisible as long as it is on the target's person. The spell ends for a target that attacks, deals damage, or casts a spell.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target one additional creeature for each slot level above 2nd.

Classes: Artificer, Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Source: Player's Handbook
