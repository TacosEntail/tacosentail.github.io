Cloud of Daggers

2nd level Conjuration

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: 60 Feet
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 Minute
Components: V, S, M (a sliver of glass)

You fill the air with spinning daggers in a cube 5 feet on each side, centered on a point you choose within range. Each creature takes 4d4 Slashing damage. A creature also takes damage if it enters the spell's area for the first time on a turn, starts its turn there, or if the spell moves into its space.

On your later turns, you can use your Action to teleport the location of the spell up to 30 feet.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 2d4 for each slot level above 2nd.

Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Source: Player's Handbook 2024
