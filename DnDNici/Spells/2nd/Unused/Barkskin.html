Barkskin

2nd level Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 Bonus Action
Range: Touch
Duration: 1 Hour
Components: V, S, M (a handful of oak bark)

You touch a willing creature. Until the spell ends, the target's skin has a rough, bark-like appearance, and the target's AC can't be less than 17, regardless of what kind of armor it is wearing.

Classes: Druid, Ranger
Source: Player's Handbook 2024
