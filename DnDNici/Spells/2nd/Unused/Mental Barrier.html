Mental Barrier (UA)

2nd level Abjuration

Casting Time: 1 Reaction, which you take when you are forced to make an INT, WIS, or CHR saving throw
Range: Self
Duration: 1 Round
Components: V

You protect your mind with a wall of looping, repetitive thought. Until the start of your next turn, you have Advantage on INT, WIS, and CHR saving throws, and you have resistance to Psychic damage.

Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Source: Unearthed Arcana 66 - Fighter, Rogue, and Wizard
