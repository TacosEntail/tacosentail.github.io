Blindness/Deafness

2nd level Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: 120 Feet
Duration: 1 Minute
Components: V

You can blind or deafen a foe. One creature that you can see within range must succeed on a CON saving throw, or be blinded or deafened (your choice) for the duration. At the end of each of its turns, the target repeats the save, ending the spell on itself of a success.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 2nd.

Classes: Bard, Cleric, Sorcerer, Wizard
Source: Player's Handbook 2024
