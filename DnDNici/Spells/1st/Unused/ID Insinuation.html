ID Insinuation (UA)

1st level Enchantment

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: 60 Feet
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 Minute
Components: V, S

You unleash a torrent of conflicting desires in the mind of one creature you can see within range, impairing its ability to make decisions. The target must succeed on a WIS saving throw or be Incapacitated. At the end of each of its turns, it takes 1d12 Psychic damage, and it can then make another WIS saving throw. On a success, the spell ends on the target.

Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Source: Unearthed Arcana 66 - Fighter, Rogue, and Wizard
