Healing Elixir (UA)

1st level Conjuration

Casting Time: 1 Minute
Range: Self
Duration: 1 Hour
Components: V, S, M (alchemist's supplies)

You create a healing elixir in a simple vial that appears in your hand. The elixir retains its potency for the duration or until it's consumed, at which point the vial vanishes.

As a Bonus Action, a creature can drink the elixir or as an Action can administer it to another creature. The drinker regainst 1d4 HP.

Classes: Warlock, Wizard
Source: Unearthed Arcana 36 - Start Spells
