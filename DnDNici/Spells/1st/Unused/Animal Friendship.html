Animal Friendship

1st level Enchantment

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: 30 Feet
Duration: 24 Hours
Components: V, S, M (a morsel of food)

This spell lets you convince a beast that you mean it no harm. Choose a beast that you can see within range. It must see and hear you. If the beast's Intelligence is 4 or higher, then the spell fails. Otherwise, the beast must succeed on a WIS saving throw or be charmed by you for the spells duration. If you or one of your companions harms the target, the spell ends.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can affect one addiational beast for each slot level above 1st.

Classes: Bard, Druid, Ranger
Source: Player's Handbook
