Burning Hands

1st level Evocation

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Self (15 Foot Cone)
Duration: Instantaneous
Components: V, S

As you hold your hands with thumbs touch and fingers spread, a thin sheet of flames shoot forth from your outstretched fingertips. Each creature in a 15 foot cone must make a DEX saving throw. A creature takes 3d6 Fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

The fire ignites any flammable objects in the area that aren't being worn or carried.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.

Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Source: Player's Handbook
