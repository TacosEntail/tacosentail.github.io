False Life

1st level Necromancy

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Self
Duration: 1 Hour
Components: V, S, M (a small amount of alchol or distilled spirits)

Bolstering yourself with a necromantic facsimile of life, you gain 2d4 + 4 temporary HP for the duration.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you gain 5 additional temporary HP for each slot level above 1st.

Classes: Artificer, Sorcerer, Wizard
Source: Player's Handbook
