Hunter's Mark

1st level Divination

Casting Time: 1 Bonus Action
Range: 90 Feet
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 Hour
Components: V

You magically mark one creature you can see within range as your query. Until the spell ends, you deal an extra 1d6 Force damage to the target whenever you hit it with a weapon attack. You also have Advantage on any WIS (Perception or Survival) check you make to find it.

If the target drops to 0 HP before the spell ends, you can use a Bonus Action to mark a new creature you can see within range.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a higher spell slot your concentration lasts longer, 2nd level (up to 4 hours), 3rd or 4th level (up to 8 Hours), 5th level or higher (up to 24 Hours).

Classes: Ranger
Source: Player's Handbook 2024
