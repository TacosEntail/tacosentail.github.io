Distort Value

1st level Illusion

Casting Time: 1 Minute
Range: Touch
Duration: 8 Hours
Components: V

You cast this spell on an object no more than 1 foot on a side, doubling the object's perceived value by adding illusionary flouish or reducing its perceived value by half with the help of illusionary dents and scratches. Anyone examing the object must roll an Investigation check against your spell DC.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a higher spell slot, you increase the size of the object by 1 foot per spell slot level over 1st.

Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Source: Acquisitions Inc.
