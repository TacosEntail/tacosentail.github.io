Expeditious Retreat

1st level Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 Bonus Action
Range: Self
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 Minute
Components: V, S

This spell allows you to move at an incredible pace. You take the Dash Action, and until the spell ends, you can take the Dash Action as a Bonus Action.

Classes: Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Source: Player's Handbook
