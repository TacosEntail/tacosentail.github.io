Puppet (UA)

1st level Enchantment

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: 120 Feet
Duration: Instantaneous
Components: V

Your gesture forces one Humanoid you can see within range to make a CON saving throw. On a failed save, the target must move up to its speed in a direction you choose or you can cause the target to drop whatever it is holding, This spell has no effect on a humanoid that is immune to being Charmed.

Classes: Bard, Warlock, Wizard
Source: Unearthed Arcana 36 - Starter Spells
