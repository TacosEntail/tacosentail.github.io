Color Spray

1st level Illusion

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Self (15 Foot Cone)
Duration: 1 Round
Components: V, S, M (a pinch of powder or sand that is colored red, yellow, or blue)

A dazzling array of flashing, colored light springs from your hand. Creatures in a 15 foot cone originating from you must succeed on a CON saving throw or have the Blinded condition unil the end of your next turn.

Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard
Source: Player's Handbook 2024
