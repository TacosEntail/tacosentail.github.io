Wild Cunning (UA)

1st level Transmutation (Ritual)

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: 120 Feet
Duration: Instantaneous
Components: V, S

You call out to the spirits of nature to aid you. When you cast this spell, choose on of the following effects:

Tracking: If there are any tracks on the ground within range, you know where they are, and you make WIS (Survival) checks to follow these tracks with Advantage for 1 hour or until you cast this spell again.

Find Food: If there is edible foliage within range, you know it and where to find it.

Find Water: If there is clean drinking water within range, you know it and where to find it.

Find Shelter: If there is suitable shelter for you and your companions within range, you know it and where to find it.

Camp Setup: Send the spirits to bring back wood for a fire and set up a campsite in the area using your supplies. The sprits build the fire in a circle of stones, put up tents, unroll bedrools, and put out any rations and water for consumption.

Camp Takedown: Have the spirits instantly break down a campsite, which includes putting out a fire, taking down tents, packing up bags, and buring any rubbish.

Classes: Druid, Ranger
Source: Unearthed Arcana 36 - Start Spells
