Beast Bond

1st level Divination

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Touch
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 Minute
Components: V, S, M (a bit of fur wrapped in cloth)

You establish a telepathic link with one beast you touch that is friendly to you or charmed by you. This spell fails if the beasts Intelligence is 4 or higher. Until the spell ends, the link is active while you and the beast are within line of sight of each other. Through the link, the beast can understand your telepathic message to it, and it can telepathically communicate simple emotions and concepts back to you. While the link is active, the best gains Advantage on attack rolls against any creature within 5 feet of you that you can see.

Classes: Druid, Ranger
Source: Xanathar's Guide to Everything
