Armor of Agathys

1st level Abjuration

Casting Time: 1 Bonus Action
Range: Self
Duration: 1 Hour
Components: V, S, M (a cup of water or shard of blue glass)

A protective magical force surrounds you, manifesting as a spectral frost that covers you and your gear. You gain 5 temporary HP for the duration. If a creature hits you with a melee attack while you have these hit points, the creature takes 5 Cold damage.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, both the temporary HP and the Cold damage increase by 5 for each slot level.

Classes: Warlock
Source: Player's Handbook
