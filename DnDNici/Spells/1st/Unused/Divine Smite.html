Divine Smite

1st level Evocation

Casting Time: Once per round, which you take immediately after hitting a target with a Melee attack
Range: Self
Duration: Instantaneous
Components: V

The target takes an extra 2d8 Radiant damage from the attack. The damage increases by 1d8 if the target is a Fiend or an Undead.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each spell slot level above 1st.

Classes: Paladin
Source: Player's Handbook 2024
