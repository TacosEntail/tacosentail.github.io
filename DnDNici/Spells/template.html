TEMPLATE

2nd level Evocation

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: 30 Feet
Duration: Instantaneous
Components: V, S, M (a red draong's scale)

A line of roaring flame 30 feet long and 5 feet wide emanates from you in a direction you choose. Each creature in the line must make a DEX saving throw. A creature takes 3d8 Fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a higher spell slot your concentration lasts longer, 2nd level (up to 4 hours), 3rd or 4th level (up to 8 Hours), 5th level or higher (up to 24 Hours).

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 2nd.

Classes: Artificer, Barbarian, Bard, Cleric, Druid, Fighter, Monk, Paladin, Ranger, Rogue, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Source: Xanathar's Guide to Everything
