Find Steed

2nd level Conjuration

Casting Time: 10 Minutes
Range: 30 Feet
Duration: Instantaneous
Components: V, S

You summon an otherworldy being that appears as a loyal steed in an unoccupied space of your choice within range. This creature use the Otherworldy Steed stat block below. If you already have a stted from this spell, the steed is replaced by the new one.

The steed resembles a Lard, rideable animal of your choice, such as a horse, camel, dire wolf, elk, or mastiff. Whenever you cast the spell, choose the steed's creature type - Celestial, Fey, or Fiend - which determins certain traits in the stat block.

Combat: The steed is an ally to you and your allies. In combat, it shares your Initiative count, and it functions as a controlled mount while you ride it (as defined in the rules on mounted combat). If you have the Incapacitated condition, the steed tatkes its turn immediately after yours and acts independently, focusing on protecting you.

Disappearance of the Steed: The steed disappears if it drops to 0 HP or if you die. When it disappears, it leaves behind anything it was wearing or carrying. You can also dismiss your steed at any time as an Action. If you cast this spell again, you decide whether you summon the steed that disappeared or a different one.

At Higher Levels. Use the spell slot's level for hte spell's level in the stat block.

Otherworldy Steed

AC 10 + 1 per spell level
HP 5 + 10 per spell level (has a number of Hit Dice [d10] equal to the spell's level)
Speed 60ft, Fly 60ft (requires level 4+ spell)

Ability Value Modifier Ability Value Modifier Ability Value Modifier
STR 18 +4 DEX 12 +1 CON 14 +2
INT 6 -2 WIS 12 +1 CHR 8 -1

Senses Passive Perception 11
Languages Telephathy 1 mile (works only with you)

Traits

Life Bond When you regain HP from a level 1+ spell, the steed regains the same number of HP if you're within 5 feet of it.

Actions

Otherworldy Slam Melee Attack Roll: Bonus equals your spell attack modifier, reach 5ft. Hit: 1d8 + the spell's level, Radiant (Celestial), Psychic (Fey), or Necrotic (Fiend) damage.

Bonus Actions

Healing Touch (Celestial, once per Long Rest) One creature within 5 feet of the steed regains a number of HP equal to 2d8 plus the spell's level.

Fey Step (Fey, once per Long Rest) The steed teleports, along with its rider, to an unoccupied space of your choice up to 60 feet away from itself.

Fell Glare (Fiend, once per Long Rest) WIS saving throw: DC equals your spell save DC, one creature within 60 feet of the steed can see. Failure: The target has the Frightened condition until the end of your next turn.
