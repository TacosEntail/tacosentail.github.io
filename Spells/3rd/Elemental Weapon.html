Elemental Weapon

3rd level Transmutation

Casting Time: 1 Action
Range: Touch
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 Hour
Components: V, S

A nonmagical weapon you touch becomes a magical waepon. Choose one of the following damage types: acid, cold, fire, lightning, poison, or thunder. For the duration, the weapon has a +1 bonus to attack rolls and deals an extra 2d4 damage of the chosen type when it hits.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot 5th or 6th, the bonus rolls increases to +2 and the extra damage increases to 3d4. When you use a spell slot of 7th or higher, the bonus increases to +3 and the extra damage increases to 4d4.

Classes: Artificer, Druid, Paladin, Ranger
Source: Player's Handbook
